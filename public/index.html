Our Mandate

Regulation Serving
All Batswana

The Botswana Communications Regulatory Authority was established under the BOCRA Act to regulate electronic communications, postal services, radio spectrum, and internet services — fostering competition, innovation, consumer protection, and national security.

  • ✓ Issue and manage communications licences and spectrum assignments
  • ✓ Protect consumer rights and enforce quality-of-service standards
  • ✓ Administer the .BW country-code top-level domain registry
  • ✓ Lead national cybersecurity coordination through BW-CIRT
  • ✓ Implement data protection and privacy regulations
  • ✓ Drive digital transformation and internet access across Botswana
